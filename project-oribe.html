About the Brand
Oribe is a high-end, luxury haircare brand known for combining professional-grade performance with luxury skincare-grade ingredients and signature fragrances. The brand caters to discerning consumers who expect both efficacy and an elevated sensory experience, people who see haircare as an extension of self-expression, not just routine maintenance.
The Question We Answered
How might we determine the balance of sustainability-driven product changes and consumer perception?

Luxury consumers hold brands to a particular standard: change too little and the brand risks irrelevance; change too much and you risk alienating the very consumers who made the brand what it is. This project sat right at that tension.
What I Did
I applied design research to brand storytelling, product packaging, and the nuanced challenge of striking the balance between luxury, security, and sustainability for a heritage brand. The work involved understanding how packaging changes are perceived not just functionally, but emotionally, because for a luxury consumer, unboxing is part of the product.
