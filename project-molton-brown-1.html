About the Brand
Molton Brown is a British luxury bath, body, and fragrance brand with over 50 years of heritage. Owned by global consumer goods giant Kao Corporation, the brand is known for its distinctive bottles, bold scents, and premium positioning in the luxury personal care space, present in everything from high-end hotel bathrooms to flagship boutiques worldwide.
The Question We Answered
How might emergent artificial intelligence tools be used to optimize digital strategy and content creation?

At a time when AI was shifting from buzzword to business reality, Molton Brown wanted to understand what was actually possible, not in theory, but in practicee. We were the team tasked with finding out.
In the Room

Our team presenting to the group of Molton Brown representatives in their New York Office

What We Did
Our team presented research findings, campaign-ready digital content, and key learnings about AI capabilities to senior leaders from Molton Brown and parent company Kao. Weeks of highly iterative experimentation and documentation yielded results that exceeded everyone's initial expectations of what was possible, and as a bonus, our team developed our own unique, transferrable process and workflow along the way.
